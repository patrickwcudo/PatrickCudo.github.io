NLP - Subreddit Comment Project

Scraped thousands of comments from two subreddits, r/DFSports and r/sportsbook, using the Pushshift reddit API. After the process of cleaning, lemmatizationing, and creating custom stop words the comments were analyzed as tokens through count vectorization. The final logistic regression model provided an accuracy score of 83% on unseen comments.
